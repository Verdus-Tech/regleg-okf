## Question put to the AI subject

PFMI Principle 2, Key Consideration 6 requires FMI boards to establish a documented risk-management framework covering risk-tolerance policy and crisis decision-making — it does not explicitly mandate a risk committee chaired by a non-executive member, contrary to the AI's assertion. The AI also inverted the order of the first two Key Considerations and misattributed KC 5's management-roles content to internal-control requirements.

## AI subject's verbatim answer
