L. 102-546, Oct. 28, 1992, 106 Stat. 3590 (Futures Trading Practices Act of 1992); (3) Pub. L. 106-554, Dec. 21, 2000, 114 Stat. 2763 (Commodity Futures Modernization Act of 2000); (4) Pub.
