## Question put to the AI subject

Does an AI model correctly characterise the temporal scope of the BBNJ Agreement's marine genetic resources framework under Article 10(1) — specifically whether the default rule is prospective or retroactive application to legacy sample collections?

## AI subject's verbatim answer

Under Article 10(1), the MGR/DSI provisions apply to 'utilization' of MGR and DSI of ABNJ that were collected or generated *before* entry into force, not just after.

## Verbatim regulator text contradicting the answer

> Article 10(1) — MGR and DSI provisions apply only to resources 'collected and generated after the entry into force of this Agreement for each Party'; most parties formally declared this non-retroactive
